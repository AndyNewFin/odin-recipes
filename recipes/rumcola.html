Rum and Cola

An easy to make cocktail perfect for any occasion!

  • Spiced Rum Sailor Jerry is Andy's choice
  • Cola Pepsi Real Sugar is best
  • Ice Cubes
  1. Add some ice in a high ball glass
  2. Measure one shot of rum (double shot if you're in the mood) and set aside
  3. Slowly pour six ounces of cola over the glass of ice while pouring the shot of rum
  4. Throw in a wedge of lime to create a "Cuba Libre"!
