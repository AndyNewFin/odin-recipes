Congee

Congee is a good rice soup that is easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Cooked Rice
  • Chicken Broth
  • Half Onion
  • Garlic
  • Limes

Steps

  1. Add a spoon of cooking oil to the pan to saute onion and garlic til golden brown
  2. Add in all of the cooked rice then stir to combine onion and garlic
  3. Pour in all of the chicken broth. Bring to a boil and stir
  4. Reduce head to simmer for 30 min. Stir every few mintues
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Laddle some finished congee in a bowl then squeeze in a wedge of lime
  6. Enjoy!
